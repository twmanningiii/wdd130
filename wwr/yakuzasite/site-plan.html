Overview

Purpose

This is a website portal for Clan Yakuza, a Destiny 2 community. This site is dedicated to the clan members and to recruting new ones. The site will provide information on who the clan is and what it can offer to perspctive clan members.

Audience

My audience is Destiny 2 players that are looking for a deeper experince within the game. Ny joining a clan they will have automatic access to raids and player Vs Player competitions that wouldn't otherwise be available to solo players. Players would be able to schedule play times with the clan and set up their own clan raids inviting any and all the clan members.
